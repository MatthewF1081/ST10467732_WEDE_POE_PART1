About Alu-Cab

Born in Africa, Built for the Wild It started with a simple desire: make it better. A plumber, tired of inadequate canopies, built his own. That spirit of innovation ignited Alu-Cab. Today, over 600 of us craft Alu-Cab gear in our Cape Town workshop, shipping to 73 countries and distributing through a network of 300 dealerships. Inspired by You, Driven by Adventure But it's not about us, because we're all about you. Driven by your wishlists and feedback, we're constantly striving to create the best lifestyle solutions available. Because your adventure deserves nothing less.

Products

There is a vast range of products that Alu-Cab offers to its customers. Here are some of the main products that they have to offer:
  • 1. Rooftop Tents: Alu-Cab is renowned for its high-quality rooftop tents that can be mounted on the roof racks of vehicles. These tents are designed to provide a comfortable and convenient camping experience, allowing users to sleep above the ground and away from insects and wildlife.

  • 2. Canopies: Alu-Cab offers a variety of canopies that can be fitted onto the back of pickup trucks. These canopies provide secure storage space for camping gear, tools, and other equipment while also offering protection from the elements.

  • 3. Roof Conversions: Alu-Cab provides roof conversion kits that allow users to transform their vehicles into versatile camping setups. These kits often include features such as awnings, storage solutions, and additional seating options.

  • 4. Campers: Alu-Cab manufactures campers that can be mounted on the back of pickup trucks. These campers are designed to provide a comfortable living space while on the road, complete with sleeping areas, kitchenettes, and storage compartments.

  • 5. ModCAP: The ModCAP is a modular camping system that allows users to customize their camping setup according to their needs. It includes various components such as tents, awnings, and storage solutions that can be easily assembled and disassembled.

  • 6. Shadow awnings: Alu-Cab offers a range of awnings that can be attached to vehicles or tents to provide shade and shelter from the sun and rain. These awnings are designed for easy setup and portability.
  • 7. Accessories: Alu-Cab also provides a variety of accessories to enhance the camping experience, including roof racks, ladders, lighting solutions, and more.

  • 8. Canopy Accessories: Alu-Cab offers a range of accessories specifically designed for their canopies, such as roof racks, storage solutions, and interior organizers.

  • 9. Rooftop Tent Accessories: Alu-Cab provides various accessories for their rooftop tents, including annex rooms, mattresses, and ventilation options.

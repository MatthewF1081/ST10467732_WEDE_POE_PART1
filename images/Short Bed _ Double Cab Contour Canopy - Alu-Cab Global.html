Information

  • 3 Built-In Lights on swivel brackets
  • Integrated Roof Rails
  • Available with or without windows
  • High-level brake light

Tailor your Double Cab with the Contour Canopy – designed to match your needs, whether you’re working hard or heading off-grid. Choose between windowed or solid panel options to control visibility and security. Integrated roof rails are ready for your gear and built-in lighting makes loading and unloading easy, even after dark. Thanks to the durable double-seal system, your cargo stays safe from dust, rain and the elements.

 

Why the Double Cab Contour Canopy?
Because no two adventures – or workdays – look the same. With customizable panels, reliable weatherproofing and smart design features, this canopy lets you shape your setup your way.

 

For the best possible installation, please note that our mounting hardware is designed to connect directly to your truck bed.  To avoid any issues and keep your warranty valid, please remove or modify your bed liner to allow for direct mounting.
